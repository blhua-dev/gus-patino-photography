Hello! It's me!

Hello everyone! I am Gus Patino! Since my childhood I have been a multimedia artist and editor. After many years of experience I decided to study various technical photography and video courses to further develop my mastery.

Along this ambitious journey, I'm working to develop astonishingly beautiful pieces of art that are seen as a mere "fantasy", essentially I strive to create those fantasies into "realities" through my craftsmanship of photography.
